About Infinite Phone

The Infinite Phone is one of the world's latest technological inovation that topples any of its rivals ranging from the iPhone to Samsung. The Infinite phone runs on "Cloud OS" with its amazing new capabilities and features and is easily affordable. This phone is highly sensor-based meaning that the phone is covered with small neuro-sensors on all sides. These neuro-sensors is what connects the phone itself to your brain, enabling the user to control the phone through their thoughts.

More Info

To avoid issues after turning on the phone, the user will receive a notification asking whether or not to turn on NOUS ( controlling the phone with your mind ). This ground-breaking ability can be accessed in the settings menu as well. NOUS is safe to the user's health tested by world famous Neuroscientist Dr. Tenzin Lodoe." NOUS is one of the few inventions that makes my mind boggle and I expected to see this kind of gadget 10 or 20 years later in the future," said the scientist.

Features

NOUS standing for "intellect" or "intelligence" is a software owned by the company of Infinite. This software is formatted on a chip known as the "N1" which receives the data of the sensors and begins proccessing the data. Ultimately commanding the phone to the user's preference. NOUS Was originally founded on January 15th,2016 in Zhenye Artificial Tech Corporation and was introduced as a feature on the Infinite Phone.
